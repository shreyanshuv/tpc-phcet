NPTEL

The National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) is an initiative by the IITs and IISc. It offers free online courses and video lectures in engineering, humanities, and management. With the goal of improving the quality of higher education in India, NPTEL ensures accessibility of quality resources to students and professionals across the country.

Infosys Springboard

Infosys Springboard is an innovative online platform aimed at fostering continuous learning and skill enhancement. This platform provides learners with access to curated content, industry certifications, and hands-on experiences, enabling them to stay ahead in the fast-paced technology world. Infosys Springboard is designed to empower students, educators, and professionals by equipping them with the skills required for the digital era.

Udemy

Udemy is a leading online learning platform offering thousands of courses in business, technology, design, and more. With expert-led video lessons and flexible, self-paced learning, it makes education accessible and affordable. Coursera

Coursera is a leading online learning platform that partners with top universities and organizations to offer courses, certificates, and degree programs in various fields. With high-quality video lectures, interactive assignments, and flexible learning schedules, Coursera provides learners with industry-relevant skills and globally recognized credentials. Whether you're looking to advance your career or explore new subjects, Coursera makes education accessible and affordable for everyone.

Simplilearn

Simplilearn is a leading online learning platform offering a wide range of courses to help individuals build essential skills for the digital economy. With over 1,500 live classes each month, it provides self-paced and live bootcamps in areas like digital marketing, data science, project management, and cybersecurity. Simplilearn partners with top universities and industry leaders to offer high-quality certifications, helping learners achieve their career goals and thrive in today's digital world.
